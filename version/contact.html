Frequently Asked Questions

How long does furniture delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days for in-stock items. Custom furniture requires 14-21 days. Free express shipping on orders above $500.
Do you offer customization?
Yes! We offer custom fabrics, wood finishes, dimensions, and colors. Our design team works with you to create bespoke furniture.
Can I visit the showroom before purchasing?
Absolutely! Our T. Nagar showroom is open Monday-Saturday 10 AM to 8 PM. Walk-ins are welcome, or you can book an appointment.
What is your warranty policy?
Xspica offers a 5-year warranty on all furniture pieces and a 100-night comfort trial. Free returns within 30 days.
